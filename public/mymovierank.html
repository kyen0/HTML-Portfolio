The Best Movies of All Time according to Cesar Ramos

My top 3 movies of all time.

Rush Hour

The comedy included in the film represents a different era in comedy cinema, as the bar of what can be said has changed drastically over the years. The perfect blend of action and comedy with a great, diverse cast makes this film a timeless classic.

The Social Network

A film that portrays the coming of the social technological age. It gives insight to how a simple project can change the fabric of social lives outside of the vast internet landscape. Along with a superb cast, this story will be a legend for ages to come.

Coco

Family movie that pulls your heart strings and captivates what it means to have true family love.
